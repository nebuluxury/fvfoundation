Why We Exist

Protecting wildlife takes all of us

We connect people with nature and empower them to take action - so wildlife thrives, ecosystems are preserved, and every child has the chance to help.

Our Mission

To protect wildlife, promote environmental stewardship, and inspire the next generation through conservation and education.

Our Vision

A world where wildlife thrives, ecosystems are preserved, and every community has the knowledge and opportunity to protect them.

Our Promise

To be their voice - guided by awareness, responsibility, and respect for all living beings, in our community and beyond.

Our Story

Founded in 1997 to give wildlife a voice

The Frank Verney Foundation was founded to inspire people of all ages to protect wildlife and natural ecosystems through conservation, education, and community engagement.

By connecting people with nature and empowering them to take action, we are building a future where wildlife thrives and every person can help be their voice.

  • Over 10,000 trees planted and multiple clean-up drives organized
  • A team of environmental scientists, educators, and activists
  • Free, hands-on programs bringing conservation to children and families
